# Tilt Rush v0.2.0

## Controls

- Corrected the Android landscape sensor-axis remapping that made both steering and forward/back tilt react in the opposite direction.
- Enabled both landscape orientations with `sensorLandscape`.

## Course additions

- Two launch ramps with airborne car physics.
- Concrete wall sections with collision response and speed loss.
- Three reusable speed-boost pads with a temporary higher top speed.
- Airborne cars can clear cones and low obstacles.
- HUD now shows READY, BOOST, or AIR status.
